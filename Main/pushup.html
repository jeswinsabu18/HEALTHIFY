Push-ups

What is a Push-up?

A push-up is a fundamental bodyweight exercise that primarily targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps while engaging multiple muscle groups throughout the body for stability and strength.

Benefits of Push-ups

Builds upper body strength and muscle

Improves core stability and posture

Enhances functional fitness

Requires no equipment

Can be modified for different fitness levels

How to Perform

1

Start in a plank position with hands slightly wider than shoulders

2

Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels

3

Lower your body by bending your elbows until your chest nearly touches the ground

4

Push back up to the starting position while maintaining proper form

5

Repeat while keeping your core engaged throughout the movement

Push-up Variations

Knee Push-ups (Beginner friendly)

Wide-grip Push-ups (Focus on chest)

Diamond Push-ups (Focus on triceps)

Decline Push-ups (Advanced)

Clap Push-ups (Explosive power)
