Squat Exercise

What is a Squat?

The squat is a fundamental strength exercise that targets multiple muscle groups, including the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and core.

Benefits of Squats

Strengthens lower body muscles

Improves core stability

Enhances flexibility and mobility

Boosts athletic performance

Helps with weight management

How to Perform a Squat

1

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.

2

Keep your chest up and engage your core.

3

Lower your hips down as if sitting back into a chair.

4

Keep your knees aligned with your toes.

5

Push through your heels to return to the starting position.
