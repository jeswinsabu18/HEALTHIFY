Plank Exercise

What is a Plank?

The plank is a core-strengthening exercise that targets multiple muscle groups, including the abs, back, shoulders, and legs. It helps improve stability and endurance.

How to Perform a Plank

1

Start in a forearm or high push-up position, keeping your body in a straight line.

2

Engage your core, glutes, and legs.

3

Keep your neck in a neutral position, looking slightly ahead.

4

Hold this position for 20-60 seconds, gradually increasing the time.

Benefits of Plank

  • Strengthens core muscles
  • Improves posture and balance
  • Enhances endurance and stability
  • Reduces back pain and enhances flexibility

Variations of Plank

  • Side Plank
  • Forearm Plank
  • High Plank
  • Plank with Leg Lift
